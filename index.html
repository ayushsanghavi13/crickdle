How to Play Crickdle

Guess the name of the cricket player who scored the given runs in the game. You have four attempts to guess the correct answer. If you get the answer wrong, the input field will turn gray, and the next input field will become available. If you guess correctly, the input field will turn green, and you will see a congratulations message.
